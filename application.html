Application Information

—— General Requirements ——

  • Must have reliable transportation to come in person to the San Jose State University campus and be picked up.
  • Must have access to a computer and a charger as we cannot provide any of these.
  • Must be able to commit to at least 8 of the 10 classes; only two absences are allowed.

—— Application Timeline ——

  • Applications are due December 14, 2024 [linked on the home page].
  • Application decisions will be sent by the first week of January.
  • We will hold virtual info sessions for accepted students in the second week of January to allow both students and parents to ask us any questions.

—— Frequently Asked Questions ——

  • Do I need any prior coding experience? No, the program is designed for beginners!
  • What materials do I need? Just a laptop with internet access is required; we’ll provide any additional resources needed.
  • Will there be homework? Most work will be done during class time, but some fun challenges will be given to explore at home.

—— Contact Us ——

If you have any questions about the application process or the program, please feel free to reach out to us at sweplusplus.sjsu@gmail.com.
