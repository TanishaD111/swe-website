The Student Experience

From day one, you’ll make new friends, learn Python coding skills, and get support from SWE++ mentors who are there to help every step of the way. By the end, you’ll walk away with a certificate, new tech skills, and the confidence to take on challenges in a field that needs more girls like you.

“Before joining SWE++ I thought that coding was all just numbers and that it would be boring. I realized how wrong I was after joining SWE++ since I learned so much from the program and I know how to use JavaScript and Python now. I can also do basic coding and create codes.” - Olivia

“At SWE++, I not only learned how to code in python, but discovered my interest in coding. While I had tried coding before, I never had a huge interest in it until I joined the SWE++ community, which provided a unique program and experience. The hands-on learning, interesting lessons, supportive environment, and most of all the amazing mentors in SWE++ work with you on your level to help you achieve your goals, and in my case, ultimately create something that I never thought would be possible- my final project: a wordle model!" - Athena
